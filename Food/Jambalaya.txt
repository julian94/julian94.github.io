Shrimp and Sausage Jambalaya
Preheat to 350   Serves 10

1 pound smoked sausage, thinly sliced
3 tablespoons olive oil
2/3 cup  chopped green pepper
2 cloves garlic, minced
3/4 cups chopped fresh parsley
1 cup chopped celery
2 - 16oz cans chopped tomatoes
2 cups chicken broth
1 cup chopped green onions
1 1/2 teaspoons thyme
2 bay leaves
2 teaspoons oregano
1 Tablespoon creole seasoning 
1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
2 cups long grain rice, washed 3 times
3 pounds raw shrimp, peeled

In 4-quart heavy bottom pot, sauté sausage, remove with slotted spoon.
Add olive oil to drippings and sauté green peppers, garlic, parsley, and celery for 5 minutes,
Add tomatoes with liquid and green onions. 
Stir in spices, rice and add sausage
cook 30 minutes, covered, over low heat, stirring occasionally, after most liquid has been absorbed by rice, add shrimp and cook until pink.
Transfer mixture to  an oblong baking dish, bake approximately 25 minutes at 350.
